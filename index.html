About CivicLens

CivicLens transforms dense parliamentary documents into plain-language summaries. Journalists, civic organizations, and citizens can track legislative decisions and community impacts quickly.

Our AI pipeline automates scraping, summarization, classification, and indexing of parliamentary reports. This ensures timely, accessible, and actionable civic intelligence at scale.
